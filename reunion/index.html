In 1988, MIT's first and only all-female a cappella group was born. Over the past 30 years, the MIT Muses have brought together over a hundred women through a shared love for STEM and music.

This December, we're excited to invite our Muses alumnae back to MIT campus for a special reunion weekend, leading up to our fall concert! Here is a general schedule of events:

8:00 - 10:00 PM Concert Description

This year, our group is larger than ever, with 19 members. In 2017, we released an album, Queen of Anything, and we're currently recording for an upcoming release in May 2019. This fall, we performed at the grand opening of MIT.nano, an EECS faculty dinner, and Math Prize for Girls! You can see what else we've been up to on our Facebook page.

We hope you'll be able to attend—we can't wait to reconnect with our alumnae (and help our alumnae reconnect with each other!)
